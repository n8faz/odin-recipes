Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies

Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies. What more can I say.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Crisco
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 1/4 Cup Flour
  • 12 oz bag Chocolate Chips
