Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies

Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies. What more can I say.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Crisco
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 1/4 Cup Flour
  • 12 oz bag Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup Crisco, 3/4 cup sugar and brown sugar to mixer and stir that real good.
  2. Add in 2 eggs and teaspoon of vanilla extract and stir.
  3. Add 1 1/4 cup flour, teaspoon of baking soda and teaspoon of salt and stir that.
  4. Add last cup of flour and continue to stir.
  5. Add chocolate chips and mix together for the final time.
  6. With the oven set at 350Â°F, place spoon sized portions of dough on tray and bake for 8-9 minutes.
  7. Place baked cookies on rack to chill.
  8. Enjoy.
