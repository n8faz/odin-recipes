Chicken Fajitas

Kroger's take on fajitas

Uses Kroger seasoning but other brands are OK. Veggie mix consists of Red and Green Peppers and Onion. Can use Steak or Beef as meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 LB thinly sliced skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 Envelope Fajita Mix
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 2 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 8 Flour Tortillas, warmed
  • 1 Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper. thinly sliced
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, thinly sliced
