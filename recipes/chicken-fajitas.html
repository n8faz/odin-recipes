Chicken Fajitas

Kroger's take on fajitas

Uses Kroger seasoning but other brands are OK. Veggie mix consists of Red and Green Peppers and Onion. Can use Steak or Beef as meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound thinly sliced skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 Envelope Fajita Mix
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 2 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 8 Flour Tortillas, warmed
  • 1 Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper. thinly sliced
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. In large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Sautee 1 onion, green and red bell pepper until tender. Remove from skillet.
  2. In same skillet, add additional 1 tablespoon oil and brown 1 pound chicken until thoroughly cooked to an internal temperature of 165Â°F.
  3. Add seasoning and 1/4 cup water; blend well. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, 3 to 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Return vegetables to skillet to heat.
  5. Place 1/2 cup filling on each of 8 warmed flour tortillas; fold. Serve immediately. Refridgerate any leftovers.
